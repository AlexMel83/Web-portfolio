The game Tic-Tac_Toe is powered by JavaScript
Tic-Tac-Toe game written in javascript. The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares. You are X, your friend is circle. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares. The first player to get 3 of her marks is the winner. When all 9 squares are full, the game is over.
