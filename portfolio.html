Smart Office and Smart Home

Smart Office introduces a workspace monitoring and management solution. With this layout, you get an interactive dashboard with systems management capability and high-level reporting of key metrics needed to efficiently and proactively manage your office. The dashboard has several states. The main state displays a list of devices, their location on the office map, and a list of their alarms. You can navigate to the device information status by clicking a row in the table. The device information depends on the device type.
